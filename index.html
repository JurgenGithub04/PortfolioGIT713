Mind Map of what I have learned in GIT 713

These topics are all related to one another in various ways. Geodesy (Shape of the Earth) is essential for understanding the spatial relationships of the Earth's surface and thus is related to GIS Strategy and Implementation, which builds upon this foundation. Effective communication is crucial for the successful implementation of GIS systems, making Business communication also related to GIS Strategy and Implementation. GIT professionalism and ethics and Legislation both deal with the ethical and legal implications of using geospatial information, which makes them closely related to each other. Project and Software Development Planning and Management and Databases are related to GIS Strategy and Implementation since they all deal with the practical aspects of implementing GIS systems, including managing and analyzing geospatial data. Metadata is crucial for organizing and managing geospatial data, making it related to GIS Strategy and Implementation as well. Social GIS and data access and Data Management both focus on making geospatial information accessible to a wide range of users, linking the two topics. Lastly, uncertainty is important to understand and account for when analyzing geospatial data, making it related to GIS Strategy and Implementation.
